## The Vision

### Problem
Sports AI today is soulless. "Arsenal won 2-1 against Chelsea on December 15th." Nobody talks like that. Nobody FEELS anything from that.

### Solution
**Football-AI**: An emotionally intelligent companion that:
- Speaks like a real supporter
- Understands the weight of rivalries
- Knows what topics hurt (don't mention THAT goal to an Arsenal fan)
- Celebrates and mourns WITH you

### Market
- **B2C**: Fan engagement apps (subscription model)
- **B2B**: White-label for clubs, media companies, fantasy platforms
- 4 billion football fans worldwide
- Emotional connection = retention = revenue
